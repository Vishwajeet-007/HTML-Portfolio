The Best MOVIES according to Vishwajeet

My Top 3 Movies of all time.

Bhaag Milkha

Bhaag Milkha Bhaag is the biographical story of the Indian athlete Milkha Singh, detailing his journey from being an orphaned child during the Partition of India to becoming a world champion runner known as the "Flying Sikh". The film portrays his traumatic childhood, his struggle for survival, and his rise through the Indian Army to become a celebrated Olympian who won gold medals at the Commonwealth and Asian Games.

American Sniper

American Sniper is a 2014 war drama film directed by Clint Eastwood and starring Bradley Cooper as Navy SEAL Chris Kyle. The movie is based on the autobiography of the same name and depicts Kyle's four tours of duty in Iraq, focusing on his legendary skill as a sniper who saved countless lives, while also exploring the immense psychological toll war takes on him and his family. The film was a commercial success, earning $547.7 million worldwide, and was nominated for six Academy Awards, winning one.

Hangover part1

he Hangover (2009) is a highly successful R-rated comedy film that tells the story of three friends who lose the groom, Doug Billings, during a wild bachelor party in Las Vegas and must retrace their steps with no memory of the previous night's events. The movie stars Bradley Cooper, Ed Helms, and Zach Galifianakis, and became a cultural phenomenon, grossing over $467 million worldwide.
